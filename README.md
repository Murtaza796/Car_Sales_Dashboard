Car Sales Dashboard (Power BI)
Project Overview

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze car sales data and generate meaningful business insights. It helps in understanding sales performance, profit trends, customer distribution, and product-level contributions using visually rich and intuitive charts.

Key Metrics (KPIs)

The dashboard highlights important business indicators:

Total Sales
Total Revenue
Total Profit
Average Order Value (AOV)

Visualizations Used & Why
1. KPI Cards
Used for: Total Sales, Revenue, Profit

Provides a quick snapshot of key business metrics for instant understanding.
2. Bar Chart (Sales by Country/Region)
   
Helps compare sales across different regions and identify top-performing markets.
3. Donut Chart (Sales by Category)
   
Shows percentage contribution of each category (Direct Sale, Pre Book, Third Party) in total sales.
4. Line Chart (Weekly/Yearly Sales Trend)
   
Tracks sales growth over time and identifies trends or fluctuations.
5. Combo Chart (Sales vs Profit by Year)
   
Combines bar and line chart to compare two important metrics:
Sales (volume)    
Profit (performance)
6. Gauge Chart (Sales vs Target)
   
Visualizes performance against target, making it easy to see if goals are met.
7. Tree Map (Profit by Sub-Category)
   
Displays hierarchical data and highlights which sub-categories contribute most to profit.
8. Stacked Column Chart (Sales by Year & Category)
   
Shows how different categories contribute to sales over time.
9. Area Chart (Sales & Profit by Year & Quarter)
   
Helps analyze trends across different time periods with better visual comparison.
10. Map Visualization (Sales by City)
    
Provides geographical insights into where sales are coming from.
11. Drilldown / Decomposition Tree

Enables deep analysis by breaking down profit based on city and other dimensions.
12. Table (Details Box)

Displays exact numerical values for detailed inspection and validation.
